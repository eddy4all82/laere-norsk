يمكنك طلب حذف حسابك وجميع بياناتك المرتبطة به من تطبيق Lære Norsk. اتبع الخطوات التالية:

الطريقة 1: من داخل التطبيق

  1. افتح تطبيق Lære Norsk

How to Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data from the Lære Norsk app. Follow these steps:

Method 1: From Within the App

  1. Open the Lære Norsk app
  2. Go to the "My Account" tab
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion request

Method 2: Via Email

Send an email to the address below, including the email address registered with your account:

e.mmnaguib@gmail.com

Data That Will Be Deleted

Data TypeAction
Account information (name, email)Permanently deleted
Profile picturePermanently deleted
Progress and achievements dataPermanently deleted
Voice pronunciation recordingsNever stored (deleted immediately after evaluation)
App settings and preferencesPermanently deleted
Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be deleted within 30 days of the request.

Data Retention Period

After confirming the deletion request:

  1. Account is deactivated immediately
  2. All personal data is deleted within 30 days
  3. No data is retained after the deletion period
